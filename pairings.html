Recommended Pairings

  • Cabernet Sauvignon – Steak, lamb, sharp cheddar

  • Merlot – Pork tenderloin, tomato-based pasta, brie
  • Syrah/Shiraz – Grilled sausage, peppercorn steak, blue cheese
